About Me

I'm a Full Stack Web App Developer based in Houston, Texas.

I have a deep passion for programming, learning, and challenging myself with new and complex ideas, while maintaining efficient and organized code that is responsive and user-friendly. I am focused on creating customizeable applications for businesses to improve productivity, customer engagement, and employee success.

After running several small businesses, and experiencing the variety of software platforms available, I have realized that not one platform is capable of satisfying an entire industry, regardless of their experience in that industry. Each business is unique in how they operate. This ranges from customer interactions and employee engagement to billing and expenses. Most small business owners are easily overwhelmed with the multitude of hats they must wear to keep their business afloat, let alone be successfull.

Understanding this dilemna first-hand, I have gathered my variety of skill sets to bring forth highly-customizeable business applications that will grow with your business and continue to be an invaluable asset to your company.
